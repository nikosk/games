# Valley of Echoes

**Existing version:** `valley-explorer.html`

## Idea

A calm exploration game about wandering through a living landscape, discovering creatures and landmarks, and restoring small parts of the world.

**Player fantasy:** Be a curious naturalist uncovering the stories of a beautiful valley.

## Main play

1. Explore an authored area.
2. Notice a creature, landmark, or environmental problem.
3. Interact and learn something about it.
4. Solve a small restoration puzzle.
5. See the area change and record the discovery.

Discovery and atmosphere matter more than world size.

## Controls

| Action | Touch | Keyboard/mouse |
|---|---|---|
| Move or pan | Drag or tap destination | WASD/arrows |
| Interact | Tap object | Space/Enter |
| Open journal | Tap journal | J |
| Zoom, if useful | Pinch | Wheel |

Choose either direct character movement or map-like panning for the first version; do not build both until play shows a need.

## Smallest fun version

- one compact authored area
- one expressive creature
- one landmark to discover
- one simple restoration puzzle
- a journal entry
- ambience and a visible change after restoration

## If it stays fun

- connected areas with distinct moods
- more creature behaviors
- weather
- traversal abilities
- hidden Echo stories
- a larger discovery journal

Do not start with a huge world or dozens of species.

## Look and sound

Painterly wilderness, gentle motion, recognizable creature silhouettes, layered wind and water, sparse music, discovery chimes, and richer sound after an area is restored.

## Tricky logic worth testing

- area connections
- discovery and journal saves
- restoration state after reload
- creature state transitions if they become complicated
- generated content only if generation is retained

## Ready for the portfolio when

A small area rewards curiosity, the restoration visibly changes the place, controls feel natural on tablet, and the scene remains smooth and inviting.
