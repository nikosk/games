# Hippo's Great Feast

**Existing version:** `hippo.html`

## Idea

A colourful culinary platform adventure where a hungry hippo travels through food-inspired places, collects ingredients, and cooks celebratory dishes.

**Player fantasy:** Be a powerful, joyful hippo eating, charging, bouncing, and cooking through a storybook food world.

## Main play

1. Run and jump through a compact stage.
2. Use one clear hippo ability to cross an obstacle or defeat a threat.
3. Collect ingredients and discover a recipe.
4. Reach the kitchen or finish point.
5. Celebrate the completed dish.

## Controls

| Action | Touch | Keyboard |
|---|---|---|
| Move | Left/right controls | A/D or arrows |
| Jump | Jump button | Space or Up |
| Ability | Context action button | E or Shift |
| Pause | Pause button | Escape |

Keep the touch layout simple. Prefer one contextual ability button over a row of permanent controls.

## Smallest fun version

- one polished food-world stage
- satisfying run and jump
- one distinctive ability, such as charge or pound
- ingredients to collect
- one recipe completion
- a playful enemy or obstacle
- strong impact, collection, and cooking feedback

## If it stays fun

- more abilities such as dive, spit, or glide
- hidden routes
- a recipe journal
- several culinary regions
- a boss that uses the abilities already proven fun

Abilities and regions are optional. Remove anything that clutters the controls or repeats another move.

## Look and sound

Round expressive hand-drawn Hippo, painted edible landscapes, bouncing and steaming food, weighty landings, crunchy impacts, happy munches, regional ambience, and a big cooking flourish.

## Tricky logic worth testing

- movement and ability states
- level data and reachable finish
- recipe and ingredient saves
- boss states if a boss is added
- old save import only if it is worth preserving

## Ready for the portfolio when

One complete stage feels great on tablet, Hippo has personality and weight, the ability serves the level, and collecting ingredients leads to a satisfying dish and ending.
