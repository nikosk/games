# Game assets

The locomotive, engine shed, station, pine trees, rocks, and pond under `images/` were generated with `bytedance-seed/seedream-4.5` through OpenRouter, then locally keyed to transparent WebP. Their final prompts are preserved under `source/`.

The short sound effects in `audio/` were generated for this game from simple synthesized tones and noise. They have no external source or license requirement.
